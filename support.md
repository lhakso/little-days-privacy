# LittleDays Support

Need help with LittleDays? You're in the right place.

The fastest way to reach us is email. We're a small independent team and we read every message.

**littledaysllc@gmail.com**

We typically reply within a couple of business days.

## What we can help with

- **Trouble signing in** or getting your memories to sync across devices
- **Questions about Little Days Plus** — the optional cloud-storage subscription (managing or cancelling it, billing questions)
- **Photobook orders** — order status, shipping, damaged or misprinted books, and refunds
- **Deleting your account or data** — or any privacy question
- **Bugs, crashes, or feature requests** — we'd love to hear them

## Managing your subscription

Little Days Plus is billed by Apple through your Apple ID. To view, change, or cancel it:

1. Open **Settings** on your iPhone.
2. Tap your name at the top, then **Subscriptions**.
3. Select **Little Days Plus** and choose **Cancel Subscription**.

Cancelling stops future renewals. Your extra storage stays active until the end of the period you've already paid for, and **no memories or photos are ever deleted** when your subscription ends.

## Photobook help

For anything about a printed book — order status, a damaged or misprinted copy, or a refund — email **littledaysllc@gmail.com** with your order details and, if it's a defect, a photo of the problem. See the [Terms of Sale](terms.html) for how orders and refunds work.

## Privacy and your data

You can edit or delete any memory from inside the app, and delete your entire account from **Settings → Delete Account**. For anything else, see our [Privacy Policy](index.html) or email us.
