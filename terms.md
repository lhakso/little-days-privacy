# LittleDays Terms of Sale

**Effective date:** 2026-06-08

These terms cover one thing: buying a printed photobook through LittleDays. Everything else in the app — writing memories, recap summaries, on-screen photobook previews — is free and isn't a "sale," so it isn't governed here. How your data is handled is covered separately in the [Privacy Policy](index.html).

LittleDays is operated by **Little Days LLC**, a small independent company, which is the seller and merchant of record for photobook orders. If anything here is unclear, or something goes wrong with an order, email us directly at **littledaysllc@gmail.com**.

## What you're buying

A LittleDays photobook is a printed, physical book made from the memories and photos you choose. Each book is printed on demand specifically for your order — it is a custom-made item, not something pulled off a shelf.

## Price

- A photobook is a **flat $34.99 USD**.
- That price includes printing and shipping within the United States.
- The price is shown to you before you pay. You are only ever charged that amount — you confirm it in the payment sheet before anything happens.

## Where we ship

At the moment, **United States shipping addresses only.** The flat price is sized for US shipping, so orders to other countries are blocked before you can pay. International shipping may come later.

## Payment

- Payment is handled by **Stripe**, using a card or Apple Pay. Your card details go directly to Stripe; LittleDays never sees or stores your card number.
- You are charged when you place the order, before the book is sent for printing.

## When your order is final

Because each book is printed to order, **an order is sent to production immediately after you pay and cannot be changed or cancelled.** There is no holding window — once payment succeeds, your book goes to our print partner (Lulu) within moments. Please double-check your photos, your book's contents, and your shipping address before you confirm payment.

The only situations where money comes back to you are the ones below.

## Refunds

- **If we can't fulfill your order**, you get a full automatic refund. If the print job is rejected or can't be produced for any reason, the system refunds your payment in full to your original payment method, with nothing required from you.
- **If your book arrives damaged or misprinted**, email us at littledaysllc@gmail.com within **30 days** of delivery, with a photo of the problem. We'll either reprint it or refund it — your choice. This covers manufacturing and shipping defects: torn pages, printing errors, binding problems, damage in transit, and the like.
- **Because books are custom-made, we don't offer refunds for buyer's remorse** — e.g. changing your mind, ordering the wrong month, choosing the wrong photos, or entering the wrong address. Check everything before you pay. If something genuinely went wrong, email us anyway — we'd rather sort it out than have you stuck with a book you can't use.

Refunds go back to the original payment method. Card networks usually take a few business days to post them.

## Delivery

- Books are printed and shipped by **Lulu**, a print-on-demand provider. To make and deliver your book, we send Lulu your book's print file and your shipping details (name, address, phone, email).
